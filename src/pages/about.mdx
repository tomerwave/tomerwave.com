## What I Do

I work at the intersection of **AI, automation, and infrastructure**. My mission is simple: let people focus on what actually matters by removing everything that gets in the way.

I'm also a **manager and entrepreneur**. I've built and led teams, launched products, and learned that great software is as much about people as it is about code.

I **speak at meetups and conferences** about engineering, AI, and building things that scale. If you're organizing an event and want me to talk, [reach out](#reach-out).

I also **mentor** software engineers, managers, and aspiring founders — whether it's navigating a career shift, scaling a team, or shipping your first product.

## Podcast

I host **Ambition Unlocked** — a podcast where I sit down with entrepreneurs and ambitious people to unpack their stories. No fluff, no filler. Just real conversations about what drives people and how they chase what they want.
